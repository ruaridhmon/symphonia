# Symphonia Logo Brief

## Brand Essence
**Symphonia** = Expert consensus synthesis platform
- Multiple voices harmonizing into insight
- Delphi method (iterative expert consultation)
- UK Government / policy audience
- Professional, trustworthy, modern

## Name Meaning
- **Symphony** = Orchestrated harmony from many instruments
- **-ia** suffix = Suggests a realm/domain (like "Academia", "Utopia")
- Core metaphor: **Many voices → One coherent output**

---

## Logo Concepts

### Concept A: Converging Waves
Multiple sound/signal waves converging into a single point or line.
- Represents: Diverse expert inputs → unified synthesis
- Style: Flowing, dynamic, suggests motion toward consensus
- Could work as animated logo (waves converging)

### Concept B: Interlocking Circles
Multiple overlapping circles forming a unified shape (like a Venn diagram center, but more elegant).
- Represents: Expert perspectives overlapping to find common ground
- Style: Geometric, balanced, suggests collaboration
- The intersection could form an "S" or other mark

### Concept C: Orchestral "S"
Abstract "S" composed of multiple parallel lines or strokes.
- Represents: Multiple voices in harmony
- Style: Elegant, premium, suggests orchestration
- Think: Stave lines from sheet music forming the letter

### Concept D: Network Nodes
Interconnected dots/nodes with one central node glowing brighter.
- Represents: Expert network with synthesis at center
- Style: Tech-forward, data-driven, suggests intelligence
- Similar energy to: Notion, Linear, Figma

### Concept E: Tuning Fork / Resonance
Abstract tuning fork shape or resonance symbol.
- Represents: Finding the right frequency/consensus
- Style: Minimal, distinctive, memorable
- Scientific/precise feeling
